Curious whether Eien.no Chat has the features you need?

Easy to use

Eien.no Chat was designed to be simple to use so that anyone can create an account and start making friends in seconds! Safe and secure

Eien.no Chats number one goal is to provide a safe place to talk to people to keep everyone connected. Modern style

We have worked hard to try and provide a nice modern design that doesn't hurt your eyes after you have been looking at it for a while. Add friends

Life is better with friends, so you can add as many as you want with no limit whatsoever. Whether you have been friends for 10 days or 10 years or live across the road or across the globe there is nothing stopping you from adding your friends and having a nice chat with them any time you feel like it. Different ways to chat
