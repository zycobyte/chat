Last updated: June 26th, 2018

Welcome!

This privacy policy is part of our Terms of Service. This page tells you what information we collect and how we store it. Any data that we collect will be used only as stated on this page. Eien.no Chat (“we”) reserve the right to modify or revise this policy at any time.

What we collect

What we collect automatically:

• We currently don’t collect any data automatically. In the future we may collect data automatically, however.

Information you provide:

• You will provide a username, password and email to create an account.
• We will store your settings and preferences
• We store anything you send to other users, such as messages, files and links

Cookies and other storage:

What we use your data for

We use your data to personalise your experience and to contact you about new messages or setting changes.
